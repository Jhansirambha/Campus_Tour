What is the Full Form of BTech?

The BTech full form is Bachelor of Technology, one of the most popular engineering courses for candidates from the PCM (Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics) branch. BTech course is a four-year-long undergraduate engineering degree programme. Various colleges and education institutions specialised in offering the B Tech course.

BTech Fees Structure

B Tech course fees differ from one university to another and mostly depend on the type of engineering college. Before enrolling in any particular college, students should check the B.Tech admission process, BTech eligibility criteria and B Tech course fees. The average B Tech course fee ranges from Rs. 4,000 to Rs. 20 Lakhs.

Why Choose BTech Course?

There are multiple reasons to choose the Bachelor of Technology programme as the undergraduate degree programme. Individuals who have an interest in the invention of things or are filled with curiosity about how instruments, machines or software are developed, can opt for the BTech degree programme. Bachelor of Technology is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge of mathematics, engineering principles, computer applications, programming and electronics. Depending on their interest, students can opt for any specialisation such as B.Tech CSE, B.Tech textile engineering, B.Tech ME, B.Tech ECE and numerous other specialisations. A BTech degree provides ample job opportunities in the field of engineering and also equips students with practical knowledge and skills for life.

BTech Eligibility Criteria

For admissions into BTech programmes, the students will have to qualify for the 10+12th examination from the science stream from any board. If a student is interested or wants to get into a B.Tech programme, it is advised to study PCM (Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics) as the main subject, as individuals from this subject group are preferred for the programme. In this section, we have mentioned the eligibility for B.Tech after 12th.

  • Candidates should have scored at least 50 per cent marks in major subjects such as Maths, Physics, Computer Science, and Chemistry, in their class 10+2 from a recognised board.
  • They need to qualify the entrance exam held by the colleges and universities you have applied for.
  • After passing the entrance examinations, candidates need to appear for the counselling process.

BTech Course Duration

BTech course duration is four years, which is divided into eight semesters. BTech course covers the introductory subjects of engineering and technology and unlike other degrees, BTech has multiple specialisation. Although students can opt for an MTech degree programme to advance their career. The four-year B Tech course covers practical classes, dissertation, project work and theories.

BTech Admission Criteria

In India, becoming an engineer is something most science students strive for. Therefore, the competition to gain BTech admission into the top engineering institutes of India is quite difficult.

  • The BTech admission process depends majorly on the entrance examination and also on the colleges students want to apply for.
  • Students who want to apply for the B.tech degree programme, need to appear for the various entrance examinations or tests, such as JEE Main, JEE Advanced, BITSAT, and AP EAMCET.
  • These entrance examinations help the institutes to admit students according to merit. Based on the performances in the above-mentioned entrance examinations, individuals are selected and further screening processes take place, before finally gaining admission to the B Tech course.
